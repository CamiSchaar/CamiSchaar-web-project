Limbus Company

Limbus Company is a game developed by Project Moon. It is a story-driven gacha game released in 2023, with new chapters, or cantos, releasing every few months. It is the third game released by Project Moon, with the previous two being Lobotomy Corporation and Library of Ruina.

Limbus Company takes place in a setting called "The City" which is run by the mysterious "Head". The City is characterized by its brutality and ruthlessness, where the strong prosper and the weak suffer. Not much is known about The Head, except that they have certain "taboo" which are basically a law of the city, and those who break these taboo are found and dealt with accordingly. The Head is seemingly all-knowing, with an unknown amount of power that they exercise over the city.

The protagonist of the game is Dante, a person with a clock for a head and missing memories of their past life. Shortly before being killed by an unknown group of assailants, they are saved by a group of twelve people called "Sinners", with two more people guiding them, a fixer by the name of Vergilius and a driver named Charon. They are contracted by a company called Limbus Company, as part of their LCB team. Each sinner made a contract with the unknown founder of Limbus Company, promising them whatever they would want. Dante joins the sinners, hoping to find some clues to their past life and missing memories, along with having to achieve the company's goal: find sources of power called Golden Boughs.
